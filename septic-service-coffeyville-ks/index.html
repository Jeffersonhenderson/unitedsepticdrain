Frequently Asked Questions

What’s the difference between septic pumping and cleaning?

Septic pumping is when we remove the liquid and floating waste from your tank. Septic cleaning goes a step further; we flush out the hardened sludge and buildup stuck to the tank walls.

Do you do emergency septic repairs?

Yes, we do. We are like your backup system; you can rely on us whenever there’s trouble. You can call us anytime, and we’ll show up fast, fix the issue, and clean up like we were never there.

How often should I pump my septic tank?

It depends on how many people use the property, but generally every 3 to 5 years is a good rule. If you’re noticing slow drains or foul smells, don’t hesitate to give us a call.

Can you install a new septic system from scratch?

Absolutely. We handle full septic tank installations. We do the digging, connect the system, and take care of the final inspection, making sure we secure all required permits in Coffeyville, KS. We’ll walk you through every step.

How long does it take your team to install a septic tank?

A typical septic tank installation can take anywhere from 1 to 5 days depending on your property, permit approvals, and weather. But we keep you updated every step of the way.

Coffeyville, KS Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Coffeyville is a city in southeastern Montgomery County, Kansas, United States, located along the Verdigris River in the state's southeastern region. As of the 2020 census, the population of the city was 8,826. Coffeyville is the most populous city of Montgomery County, and the home to Coffeyville Community College. The town of South Coffeyville, Oklahoma is approximately 1 mile south of the city.
